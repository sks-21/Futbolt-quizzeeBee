Rules
  1. There are 10 questions in the quiz.
  2. All questions in the quiz are of multiple choice with a single correct answer.
  3. For each correct answer you will be awarded +3 points.
  4. For each wrong answer you will be awarded -1 points.
  5. Each question has a timer, so be alert !
  6. You may skip some questions and come back later to try it again !
  7. Most importantly, focus on enjoying the quiz rather than scoring high !!
